The tendency that a person acts in an organized or spontaneous way. You prefer to be careful and thorough with your decisions. It is better to play safe than sorry, and take comfort in security.

The extent to which a person’s emotions are sensitive to the environment. It measures how you react to emotional stimuli, as well as your readiness of defense for yourself and your assets. You are reactive to changes in the market.
